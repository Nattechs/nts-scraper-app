About NTS Scraper

Learn more about our powerful email and phone number extraction tool

Our Mission

To provide businesses and individuals with a powerful, user-friendly tool for extracting contact information from websites.

NTS Scraper was developed by NATTECHS NG to address the common challenge of finding contact information on websites. Whether you're a sales professional looking to build a prospect list, a researcher collecting data, or a marketer building an outreach campaign, NTS Scraper simplifies the process of gathering emails and phone numbers from websites.

Our tool is designed with both simplicity and power in mind. The intuitive interface makes it easy for anyone to use, while the advanced scanning capabilities ensure you get comprehensive results. With features like whole-website scanning, customizable settings, and multiple export options, NTS Scraper is the ideal solution for all your contact information extraction needs.

We are committed to continuous improvement and regularly update our tool with new features and enhancements based on user feedback. Our goal is to make NTS Scraper the most reliable and efficient contact information extraction tool available.

Key Features

Email Extraction

Quickly find and extract email addresses from any website with our powerful scanning algorithm.

Phone Number Detection

Identify phone numbers in various formats from websites, even when they're embedded in text or images.

Whole Website Scanning

Scan entire websites, not just individual pages, to ensure you don't miss any contact information.

Customizable Settings

Tailor the scanning process to your specific needs with our comprehensive settings options.

Multiple Export Formats

Export your results in various formats including CSV, JSON, and Excel for easy integration with other tools.

Fast Performance

Get results quickly with our optimized scanning engine, even when processing large websites.

How It Works
Step 1: Enter Website URL

Start by entering the URL of the website you want to scan. Make sure to include the full address including http:// or https://.

Step 2: Configure Scan Options

Choose what type of information you want to extract (emails, phone numbers, or both) and whether you want to scan just the homepage or the entire website.

Step 3: Start Scanning

Click the Scan button to begin the extraction process. Our tool will navigate through the website, analyzing the content for contact information.

Step 4: Review Results

Once the scan is complete, you'll see a list of all the emails and phone numbers found, along with their source pages.

Step 5: Export or Copy

You can copy individual results, copy all results at once, or export the data in your preferred format for use in other applications.
